About Me

My professional journey began in Industrial Manufacturing, where I gained hands-on experience ranging from selling Sheet Metal and Iron Worker Machinery with Tooling to installing Industrial Robots and managing Automotive Paint Shop Operations. Over the years, this diverse experience has helped me develop a strong foundation in Manufacturing Technology, Automation, Maintenance Engineering, and Operational Excellence.

Currently, I serve as Deputy Manager – Paint Shop Operations at Honda Cars India Ltd., with responsibilities spanning Plant & Machinery Maintenance, Robotic Automation, Process Optimization, and Continuous Improvement initiatives. My expertise includes Automotive Painting Processes, ABB Robotics, Industrial Automation, and Manufacturing Excellence.

Alongside my engineering career, I have completed the BS Degree in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras and continue expanding my knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Large Language Models (LLMs). My vision is to bridge the gap between Manufacturing and Artificial Intelligence to create Smart Factories, Intelligent Production Systems, and Data-Driven Industrial Solutions.
